Background

We all believe that a vision is not a vision until it's proved by sight that is a vision. We were not on the map until we made it clear for all sight to see. Thereafter, the company came after an afterthought to provide green and environmentally friendly energy resources. It came as a solution giver to the Zambian power crisis and excessive load shedding as well as escalating fuel prices for power supply.
The company was incoporated in the year 2024 as AKIT INVESTMENTS (PVT) LTD under the compaines Act 2017 (Act No 10 of 2017) and it has complied with all regulatory authorities, PACRA with TPIN 2003099411.
The company is owned by Khulekani Mafoshola, Andrew Simutowe and Tinashe Ishbar Sakarombe, the directors who are also vissionaries of the company and Tinashe Ishbar has worked in various organizations including ZIMASCO, and POWERITE Zimbabwe to mention just a few, as an electrical engineer first grade.
